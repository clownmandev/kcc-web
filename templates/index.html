Kindle Comic Converter (Web)

Step-by-Step for Kindle Paperwhite 6th Gen:

  1. Select Kindle Paperwhite 1 & 2 above.
  2. Select MOBI format.
  3. Click Convert and wait for the download.
  4. Connect your Kindle to PC via USB.
  5. Drag the downloaded file into the documents folder on the Kindle drive.
